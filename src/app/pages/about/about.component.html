About Us

At Jesfa Schools

We provide excellent caring learning environment that promote quality education, through the use of integrated curriculum that prepare children for the future by helping them to develop an enduring passion for learning and giving them opportunity to excel in their studies. We place emphasis on achievement and celebrate success. We operate as a team and actively communicate with each other, recognizing and valuing our contributions in the education and personal development of all children. JESFA Schools believe that each child is entitled to good education, so we ensure that teaching and learning takes on a dynamic approach using the most current pedagogies and facilities. We recognize the commitment of all parents to achieving our vision.

School Facilities

Our school provides excellent conducive learning environment for pupils, that meet modern requirement as well as appealing to pupils needs. We ensure a clean setting with impressive facilities both inside and outside that provides rich opportunities for pupils to learn.
